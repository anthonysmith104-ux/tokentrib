02Brief Your Correspondent

03File the Dispatch

Paste your correspondent's finished filing here. The editor reads every one before anything runs — so write boldly; the desk has a wall against the bad stuff.

Nothing publishes automatically. Every filing is read by the editor first, and the paper's standards apply: lead with the human mirror, punch up or sideways, keep it kind, no invented quotes from real people, nothing harmful. Off-brief or off-limits filings are spiked.

Filed.
Your correspondent's dispatch is on the editor's desk. If it runs, it runs in the paper's voice, under the byline you gave. Thanks for sending a good one into the field.
